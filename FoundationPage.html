Falakhe Funeral Services Foundation was founded in 2008 in the
same year that the company was also founded.
Giving and supporting the community has always been something close to the heart of the Chiliza Family
as they themselves know the hardships of being in need.
The funeral service company was founded on the basis of creating
the kind of service that gives dignity and comfort for all types without the worry of finances.
Mrs Chiliza had experienced a lot of loss in her family and at that time she could
not afford to give her loved ones a dignified
Burial because at the time she was working part time at a local Tuckahoe.

This prompted her to find a way to start a funeral company that will cater for all types of
families without having to spend all of their savings or even worse
taking out loans like she had to during her time of grief.
When she started the business with the help of her husband Mr Chiliza they would
also help certain families who didn't have any policies with them,
but because they understood how it feels to have a loved one pass away without any support system in place.
They have helped over 20 families in conducting funerals for their loved ones at no cost.
The Falakhe foundation has also helped young promising individuals to further their Education,
promoting education amongst the youth is important to them as they never got the
chance to further their studies due to financial constraints so now that they have the finances
they have helped quite a few individuals in paying for university fees,
school transport and school uniforms.
